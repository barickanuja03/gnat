About GNAT

GNAT Technologies Pvt. Ltd. is a company, specializes in Non-Destructive Testing services in the field of Aerospace, Defence and General Engineering Industry sectors comprising of NDT Level-III Consultancy Services, Training Qualification & Certification Services of NDT personnel accordance with NAS 410 & EN 4179, Independent NDT Test lab capabilities, Sale & Calibration of NDT accessories.

GNAT Technologies Pvt. Ltd. is accredited by Aerospace Prime Contractors to provide Nondestructive Testing services to their Subcontractors and Suppliers. GNAT Technologies has the capability to carry out Independent Nondestructive Testing using the methods of Liquid Penetrant, Magnetic Particle, Radiography, Eddy-Current, and Ultrasonic Testing.
